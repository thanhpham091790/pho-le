Welcome

Located in the heart of Denver, Colorado, Pho Le is an authentic Vietnamese restaurant where you can enjoy amazing and unique dishes from one of the most beautiful countries in South East Asia while you are not putting your feet on Vietnam’s territory.

We serve delicious, exquisite, and best-loved Vietnamese dishes, such as Pho, rice noodle bowls, spring rolls, steam rice plates, new, special dishes, and much more. With Pho Le’s well prepared food, nice clean fresh atmosphere, friendly, efficient service, you will like our restautant from the minute you walk in. Thank you for your attention to us, we hope to see you soon in Pho Le.
